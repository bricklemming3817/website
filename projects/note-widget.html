noteapp

a minimal tool that keeps ideas within reach, making notes visible, simple, and part of daily life.

what

noteapp is a lightweight note taking app that keeps the right ideas in sight. quick to add, easy to edit, nothing extra.

why

notes shouldn’t hide behind taps. keeping a small set of ideas in sight helps you focus on what matters.

how

  • capture ideas in a single view
  • pin important notes on top
  • see one note at a glance on your home screen
  • clean layout with no clutter
  • hidden folders — less clutter only what’s relevant upfront
