Résumé Generator

Opinionated generator for clear, useful résumés.

clarity utility
See the why

Purpose: Reduce editing time and remove fluff.

Why: Clarity is kind; readers have limited time.

Approach: Schema-first content, tight templates, lint rules.

Outcome: Edit time cut ~60%; ATS-safe output.

Reflection: Good constraints produce better writing.

Philosophy

  • Build for clarity → schema-first content and tight copy.
  • Reduce surface area → fewer settings, fewer errors.
  • Ship small, iterate → reality beats speculation.
  • Make time costs visible → fewer clicks, faster feedback.
  • Leave it better → clean diffs, clear docs, simple exits.
